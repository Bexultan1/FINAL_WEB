About us

Tapki is an online home footwear store that offers a wide selection of slippers for men, women and children.
We strive to provide our customers with the best quality and most comfortable shoes at affordable prices. We are confident that you will be able to find the slippers that are perfect for you in our store.
Thanks for choosing us!





In the creation of our shoes we use only high quality and comfortable materials, as we value the trust of our customers. In our factory, we use only the best materials like :

  • Leather: it is strong, durable and has a good appearance. Various types of leather can be used, including genuine leather, suede and exotic leather (e.g. crocodile or snakeskin).
  • Textile: Textile materials such as cotton, linen, nylon and polyester are used to create various types of shoes including sneakers, sneakers and cloth shoes. Textiles provide good ventilation and are lightweight.
  • Rubber: Rubber soles are standard on most shoes as they provide good traction and cushioning.
  • Plastic: Plastic materials can be used to create various parts of the shoe, including plastic inserts, buckles, and decoration details.
